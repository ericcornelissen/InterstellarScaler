Interstellar Scaler

This project by Eric Cornelissen is a simple web app that you can use to find out what you'll weigh on another planet! Feel free to use it for whatever you want and enjoy your stay on this site. And if you're a geek like me, you can also check out the source code on Github.

For all the science people (and those who are interested), I know that your mass does not actually change... The thing that changes when you go to a different planet is de amount of force pulling you towards the ground. This phenomenon is used by a balance to show you what you weigh. So, the amount of force changes and your mass stays the same.
